#
##Project overview

###This project
    This project was conducted for learning purpose and to show how data-driven marketing can be made.

###Data source
    The project is based on fictional and simplified data,created manually in Excel.It concerns data sales of a coffe shop, covering period of 17 days.

##Tools
### Excel
### MySQL
### GoogleAds

##Methodology
    Fictional sales data was generated using Excel,queried and aggregated in MySQL,includes date,
    product names,price,quantity,customer type,channel type. Campaign planning was 
    simulated in Google Ads.

##Key Insights
    - Sales revenue from 1 December to December 19 was 3.276
    -The top-performing product was Latte with revenue of 975.00
    generating 30% of total revenue
    -Returning customers provided lower revenue than new ones,which
    indicates that tergeting new customers is effective
    -Offline sales generated higher revenue than online,focus on
    promoting physical location
